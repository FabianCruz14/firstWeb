trendingVol

trendingVol is an open-source app that allow you to analyze volleyball games with just a few clicks the main idea is to be able to calculate statics in a simple way and within reach of most users.

It arises as an idea from the needs of the author to know the errors and successes of his volleyball team, we know that the best positioned programs in the market are expensive and inaccessible to most coaches and athletes, we think of them.

It is designed to meet the needs of high-performance teams because we are aware that good data analysis is a great tool when competing at the highest level.
